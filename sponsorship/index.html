Opportunities

Sponsorship Opportunities

We'll work with you to build a package that fits your goals and budget.

On-Air

15 or 30-second sponsorship messages on 88.5 FM and our regional network. Read in a straightforward, non-commercial tone. You can sponsor specific programs (like World Cafe), dayparts, or run messages throughout the day.

WXPN airs only 4 sponsor messages per hour vs. 22 ads on commercial radio. Your message isn't lost in clutter.

Digital

Reach listeners online through multiple channels:

  • Streaming audio — Pre-roll messages when people start our live stream (web, app, smart speakers)
  • Website display ads — Banners on xpn.org, The Key blog, concert calendar, playlists
  • Email newsletters — Banner placement in our weekly music updates and concert alerts
  • Sponsored content — "Presented by" opportunities for playlists, concert calendar, special features
  • Podcast/on-demand — Sponsor archived World Cafe episodes and live sessions

Events

WXPN hosts dozens of events each year. Sponsor opportunities range from on-site presence to naming rights:

  • XPoNential Music Festival — Our annual summer festival drawing thousands of music fans
  • Free at Noon — Weekly free concerts (in-person and virtual)
  • Musicians On Call 5k — Charity run supporting music therapy
  • Special concerts and community events — Year-round opportunities

Custom Packages

Most sponsors combine on-air, digital, and event elements for wider reach. We'll build something that makes sense for your goals and budget.
